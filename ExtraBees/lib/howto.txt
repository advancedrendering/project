1. open properties of eclipse project
2. go to "Java Build Path"
3. select tab "Libraries"
4. click "Add External JARs"
5. select jogl.jar and click okay
6. expand jogl.jar entry
7. double-click "Native library location"
8. select the subfolder of jogl-natives which fits your architecture (Linux 64bit at F1 pool)
9. repeat steps 4-8 for the gluegen-rt-library
